My eBay business has been a remarkable experience that has taught me an extraordinary amount about business development through the design of efficient systems and leadership skills from the effective management of 3 employees and a freelancer. The 3 employees (who are friends) take hold of all the inventory and are in charge of shipping out packages for all orders. This means that I can continue to have a profitable and lucrative business without having to spend as much time on it. I now spend an average of about 30 minutes or less on the business while maintaining monthly sales of about $8,000 to $12,000 with around 500 to 700 items sold.

Being critically analytical of the processes in my business has taught me to be more analytical of myself.
